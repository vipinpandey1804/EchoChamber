## üëª Role of Echoes
Echoes are **strategic helpers** that can move the exit location:

### What Echoes Do:
- **Press Enter** to create a "helpful" echo at your current position
- Echoes replay your actions after a 3-8 tick delay
- When echoes activate (every 10 ticks), they can **relocate the green exit**
- Use echoes to move the exit closer to you if you're stuck

### Echo Types:
- **Helpful (Cyan)**: Creates breadcrumb trails, moves exit strategically
- **Chaotic (Magenta)**: Unpredictable behavior, random exit movement
- **Mirror (Yellow)**: Reflects your movements in opposite direction
- **Delayed (Orange)**: Builds up energy then releases

### Strategy:
- Create echoes near walls or dead ends
- Wait for echoes to activate and potentially move the exit
- Each echo gives +10 points to your score
